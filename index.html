The Challenge

The owners of Mountain View Chalet had a stunning property but were completely dependent on Airbnb for bookings. They were losing a significant portion of their revenue to commissions, had no direct relationship with their guests, and spent countless hours manually managing inquiries.

The Solution

Fullyo was brought in to build a complete, independent business system. Our four-pillar approach included: 1. A visually stunning website with an integrated booking engine. 2. Optimizing their existing Airbnb listing and syncing it with the new site. 3. Implementing a PMS to automate guest messaging. 4. Launching a targeted social media campaign to drive direct traffic.

The Results

Within six months, the new website became their primary source of revenue, dramatically increasing profitability. The automation freed up the owners' time, allowing them to focus on guest experience and acquiring new properties. The Chalet is now a thriving, independent brand.
